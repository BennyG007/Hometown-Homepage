Visit Sanford

Known as the "Historic Waterfront Gateway City", Sanford is home to Seminole Sate College and the Central Florida Zoo and Botanical Gardens.

Top three activites to do in Sanford

Attend a show or a concert

Catch a local production of your favorite show in this intimate historic setting.

Visit a local brewery

Enjoy a local brew and some great food at a local staple of Sanford.

Go for a sunset boat ride

Enjoy the susnet while you cruise the St. Johns river along with food and drinks.
